Real-World Example

Restaurant QR Menu System

Each table at a restaurant has a unique QR code that customers can scan to:

  • View an interactive menu with videos of each dish
  • See detailed descriptions, ingredients, and pricing
  • Place orders directly through the platform
  • No app download required - works directly in browser

Restaurant owners can easily update menu items, prices, and availability in real-time through their dashboard.
